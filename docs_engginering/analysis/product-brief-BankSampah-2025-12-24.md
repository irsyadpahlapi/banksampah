## Executive Summary

BankSampah adalah **platform digital paling mudah digunakan** untuk mengubah sampah menjadi aset berharga - di mana setiap orang Indonesia bisa dengan mudah, transparan, dan bangga berkontribusi pada ekonomi sirkular yang nyata.

### The Opportunity

Indonesia menghadapi krisis sampah dengan **27,000 bank sampah** dan **650,000 nasabah** yang masih beroperasi secara manual, tidak transparan, dan memberikan value ekonomi yang rendah. Sementara itu:

- **Government urgency**: KLHK target 30% waste reduction by 2025 (sudah terlambat), regulasi DKI Jakarta wajib pisah sampah dengan denda
- **Climate crisis**: Indonesia = 2nd largest ocean plastic polluter, sampah memperburuk banjir Jakarta
- **Economic potential**: Rp 50.5 triliun market jika 27K units terdigitalisasi dengan marketplace (Rp 1.87B per unit potential)
- **Social momentum**: Post-pandemic behavioral shift, generasi muda dengan climate anxiety tinggi menginginkan action konkret

### The Problem

**"Masyarakat Indonesia ingin berkontribusi pada lingkungan bersih dan berkelanjutan, tapi sistem bank sampah saat ini TIDAK MEMBERIKAN PENGALAMAN YANG MUDAH, TRANSPARAN, DAN REWARDING - sehingga motivasi awal cepat hilang dan sampah kembali ke TPA."**

**Breakdown masalah spesifik:**

1. **Friction tinggi**: Harus datang ke bank sampah fisik, jam operasional terbatas, antri
2. **Opacity**: Tidak tahu harga fair, tidak tahu impact sebenarnya, tidak tahu ke mana sampah mereka
3. **Low perceived value**: Dapat Rp 5,000 setelah kumpulin sampah sebulan = not worth the effort
4. **No habit formation**: Tidak ada sistem yang bikin sorting & depositing jadi habit
5. **Isolated experience**: Tidak ada community, tidak ada comparison, tidak ada recognition

**Result**: Low engagement (1-2 transaksi per bulan), high dropout rate, dan waste management yang tidak sustainable.

### The Solution

Platform digital yang menggabungkan **ledger system + marketplace ecosystem + gamification** untuk menciptakan circular economy yang complete dan rewarding.

**3-Phase Evolution:**

**Phase 1 (Year 0-1): Foundation - Nasabah â†’ Bank Sampah**

- Core transaction infrastructure dengan dual ledger (kg + rupiah)
- Gamification & habit building (badges, levels, daily impact tracking)
- Progressive UX (4 levels: simple onboarding â†’ power user features)
- **Goal**: Prove unit economics & user retention

**Phase 2 (Year 1-2): Marketplace - Bank Sampah â†’ Buyers/Industry**

- Buyer-supplier matching dengan price transparency
- Dynamic pricing with price protection mechanisms
- Bulk ordering & logistics optimization
- **Goal**: Unlock Rp 1.87B GMV potential per cluster

**Phase 3 (Year 2-3): Circular Economy - Industry â†’ Products â†’ Nasabah**

- Partnership dengan brands untuk "Made from Your Waste" products
- Traceability dengan blockchain/QR code: "Botol plastik kamu sekarang jadi tas ini"
- Closed loop rewards: Beli produk recycled = bonus points untuk deposit berikutnya
- **Goal**: Complete circular narrative - emotional & tangible proof

## Core Vision

### Vision Statement

**"Menjadi platform digital paling banyak digunakan untuk mengubah sampah menjadi aset berharga - di mana setiap orang Indonesia bisa dengan mudah, transparan, dan bangga berkontribusi pada ekonomi sirkular yang nyata."**

### Mission

Memberdayakan masyarakat Indonesia untuk menciptakan lingkungan bersih dan berkelanjutan melalui:

- **Financial empowerment**: Fair pricing & transparent marketplace
- **Knowledge empowerment**: Education, gamification, waste sorting mastery
- **Identity empowerment**: Environmental hero positioning & community recognition
- **Community empowerment**: Collective action & neighborhood impact

### Problem Statement

#### The Core Problem

Existing bank sampah systems fail to create sustainable behavior change because they:

- Make recycling **difficult** (physical visits, limited hours, manual processes)
- Keep value **opaque** (unclear pricing, unknown impact, untraceable waste flow)
- Provide **low rewards** (Rp 5,000/month doesn't justify effort)
- Lack **habit mechanisms** (no gamification, reminders, or progress tracking)
- Offer **isolated experiences** (no community, benchmarking, or recognition)

#### Problem Impact

**For Nasabah (650,000 existing + millions potential):**

- Initial motivation to recycle quickly fades
- Feel their contribution is meaningless
- Return to throwing everything in trash
- Miss economic opportunity (waste = underpriced asset)

**For Bank Sampah Operators (27,000 units):**

- Manual record-keeping = errors and inefficiency
- Cannot scale beyond local neighborhood
- Miss out on marketplace premium pricing
- Limited visibility for government/donor reporting

**For Environment & Society:**

- Only 7-8% national waste gets recycled (KLHK data)
- Indonesia = 2nd largest ocean plastic polluter globally
- Waste contributes to Jakarta floods and health issues
- Missing Rp 50.5 trillion economic potential from waste

**The Burning Platform** - Why NOW is critical:

1. **Regulatory Urgency**: KLHK 30% reduction target overdue, Jakarta mandatory waste separation with fines (2024)
2. **Climate Existential Threat**: Ocean plastic crisis, urban flooding, generational anxiety demanding concrete action
3. **Economic Window**: First-mover advantage in fragmented Rp 50T+ market before dominant player emerges
4. **Social Momentum**: Post-pandemic digital behavior shift + climate consciousness = perfect adoption timing

### Why Existing Solutions Fall Short

#### BankSampah.id (Market Leader - 6,294 units)

**Strengths**: Government backing, national integration, offline capability  
**Fatal Gaps**:

- Government-centric = poor end-user experience for nasabah
- Basic ledger only = no investment mindset or value appreciation
- No marketplace = missed premium pricing opportunity
- Slow innovation = large organization bureaucracy

#### Waste4Change (Corporate Leader - 200+ B2B clients)

**Strengths**: Credibility, full-service operations, international partnerships  
**Fatal Gaps**:

- B2B only = does not serve community/individual nasabah
- Premium pricing = unaffordable for grassroots bank sampah
- Operations-heavy = not scalable digital platform
- Corporate CSR focus = not economic empowerment for masyarakat

#### MallSampah (Consumer Traction - 30K users)

**Strengths**: Mobile UX, on-demand pickup, low barrier to entry  
**Fatal Gaps**:

- Transactional only = no savings or ledger system
- Individual-centric = not community bank model
- No circular economy vision = collection stops at recycling
- Limited scale = 30K users vs. 650K existing bank sampah users

**CRITICAL INSIGHT**: No competitor combines sophisticated ledger + marketplace + circular economy. This is our **blue ocean opportunity**.
